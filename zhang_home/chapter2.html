03Weather Condition Impact

The relationship between temperature and humidity and traffic accidents and it in different time period.

Can you find out which temperature had the highest number of accidents in November 2019? How about the humidity?

Diagram Guide

When the mouse moves over the bubble, detailed information is displayed. When you move the mouse over the gamut slider, you can move the mouse to see the data distribution at the corresponding time. You can also select different years in the drop-down box, so that the distribution of the corresponding years will appear.

Statement

In the analysis of temperature and humidity in weather conditions, we can also see that weather is a factor affecting traffic accidents.

Accidents are more likely to occur when temperatures are around 60°F. When temperatures are higher or lower, fewer accidents occur. This could be because temperature affects people's willingness to travel.

For humidity, when humidity is higher, traffic accidents are more likely to happen. This may be because the high humidity is generally rainy or snowy days, when the road will be more humid, easy to skid. And such weather visibility will be relatively low, affecting the sight of driving, so it is more prone to traffic accidents.

Statement

In the analysis of road conditions, we can see that road conditions are a factor affecting the severity of traffic accidents. When road conditions are extremely bad, traffic accidents can easily lead to fatal injuries. Bad road conditions can lead to moderate accidents. And most road conditions generally only cause minor accidents.

In addition, traffic accidents differ in gender distribution only for moderate injuries. There was no difference in the distribution of other traffic accidents.
